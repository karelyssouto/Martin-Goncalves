About Martin

Martin Goncalves is a 17 year old, Caracas-based photographer who started to share his work on a instagram page that he created to showcase his work and his passion for photography. He lives in Caracas, Venezuela and part of his mission is to tell stories through picture. "A picture says more than a thousand words", he quotes when he explains the motives behind his photography.
